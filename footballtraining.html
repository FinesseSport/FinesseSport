Finesse Sport United FC

The Philosophy

At Finesse Sport United FC we believe every player should play with confidence and belief. Playing with a high level of intelligence and having the freedom to be creative will allow football to be enjoyable, exciting and entertaining. Through this, players will develop geunine passion and desire for the sport, which are all the attributes that contribute to success.

We aim to adopt a fluid and exciting attacking playing style that combine the principles of total football, high intensity, possession and ball retention developed by Jhna Cruyff and associated with the likes of some of the world best football teams like Liverpool FC, Manchester City FC, AFC Ajax and FC Barcelona. We are major advocates for individuals to play with speed, flare and skills; being courageous and bold with their approach to the beautiful game. These are components associated with African and South American players such as Messi, Pogba, Ronaldinho and Neymar




The Coaches


All coaches are highly competent and experienced with their main focus to make the players their main priority. We encourage positive attitude and outstanding behaviour amongst children. This ensures the highest quality of teaching coaching, mentoring and learning are all times. As we strongly believe that a positive attitude is the foundation for successful learners. Finesse Sport coaches have experience working with children and are screened by having an enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS).

1-On-1 Training





Whether your an aspiring football player, newcomer to the game or would like to build up your confidence, Finesse Sport can provide this. Our 1-On-1 training is personalised to each individual and focuses on what areas they would need to improve. Finesse Sport will help your child improve their technique, speed, flexibility, power and agility with and without the ball. Players have the power to choose, what day and time they would like to train.
