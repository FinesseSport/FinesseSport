Community and youth engagement is very important to us at Finesse Sport. We aim to have a big impact in our local community by positively influencing the lives of everyone we connect with. Barking & Dagenham is a wonderful borough full of potential and hidden gems. It is one of the greenest boroughs in London with great open spaces and play areas. These spaces make up approximately 33% of the borough but they are being under utilised. One of our objectives is to encourage local residents to be physically active in these spaces. Not just using these spaces but taking ownership their local area whilst doing traditional or unconventional activities in them.
